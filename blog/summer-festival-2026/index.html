Anguilla Summer Festival 2026 — The Complete Guide

Published on July 17, 2026 by Roam Anguilla Team | Festivals & Events

The Anguilla Summer Festival is back for its 52nd annual summer celebrations. A grand showcase of Anguilla's best in cultural arts and entertainment culminating in multiple unforgettably electric summer parties and celebration. And this year is shaping up to be one for the history books.

Carnival Season Is Here

From the electric chaos of J'Ouvert morning to the elegance of the Miss Anguilla Pageant, the thunder of boat racing to the pure bacchanal of the Caribbean Beach Party — this is the island's biggest celebration of culture, community, and pure Caribbean joy. And here's the best part: event ticket prices have never been this affordable , make it the most accessible and most event-packed festival season to date!

So Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned fetter mapping out your fete schedule, we've put together everything you need to know.

Carnival Parties

The Anguilla summer festival sees its fair share of public street and beach parties. August Monday is fameously known for its 24 hour party spirit. Starting in The Valley, the island's centrally located capital, J'ouvert begins in the wee hours the early morning and gradually grows into a sea of thousands all partying towards the culminating point, Sandy Ground.
