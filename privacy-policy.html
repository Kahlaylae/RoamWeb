Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

Roam Anguilla (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the application uses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Roam Anguilla does not require users to create an account and does not collect or store personal information on our own servers.

The application accesses your device’s location only after you grant permission through your device’s operating system. Your location is used solely to provide location-based features within the app.

Third-party services used by the application, such as Google AdMob, may collect certain information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Location Data

If you grant location permission, your location is used only while the application is in use to:

  • Display nearby places, attractions, and businesses.
  • Calculate distances to points of interest.
  • Provide relevant location-based content and features.

Roam Anguilla does not store your location on our own servers, create a history of your movements, or sell your location information.

You may revoke location permission at any time through your device’s Settings.

Advertising

Roam Anguilla displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google and its advertising partners may collect certain information to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements, depending on your device settings and applicable laws.

For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Information about Google AdMob:

https://admob.google.com/

We do not control the information collected directly by Google through its advertising services.

Information We Do Not Collect

Roam Anguilla does not intentionally collect or store:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Messages

unless you voluntarily provide such information by contacting us via email.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information collected by Google through its advertising services is governed by Google’s own Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to safeguard information processed by the application. However, no method of electronic transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

Roam Anguilla is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You may disable location access at any time through your device’s Settings.

You may also limit ad personalization through your device or Google Account settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date above. Continued use of the application after any changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
